You Can't Stop The Rhythm - A musical revue celebrating iconic shows across the decades, brought to life through medleys that capture the very best of each musical.

We’re looking for strong, confident vocalists who can sing well in harmony and work well as part of a team. Featured solos available, but strong ensemble players are essential. All ages welcome.

We also need expressive, high-energy dancers to front the movement side of the show. Singing not essential, ensemble vocals may be required. Adults only.

We're looking to have a cast of up to 30 people. Up to 10 of the cast will be from The Archway Theatre Youth Workshop.

Please prepare 1 minute – 90 seconds of a song of your choice. We would like you to sing to a backing track (your phone can link to our speaker via Bluetooth) Lots of options for tracks can be found on YouTube. We understand that some of our younger youth workshop members may not have a phone, if this is the case then please could parents send their backing tracks to one of us in advance.

We look forward to seeing you there!

- Laura and Hannah
