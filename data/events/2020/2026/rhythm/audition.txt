You Can't Stop The Rhythm - A musical revue celebrating iconic shows across the decades, brought to life through medleys that capture the very best of each musical.

We’re looking for strong, confident vocalists who can sing well in harmony and work well as part of a team. Featured solos available, but strong ensemble players are essential. All ages welcome.

We also need expressive, high-energy dancers to front the movement side of the show. Singing not essential, ensemble vocals may be required. Adults only.

We're looking to have a cast of around 30 people. Around 10 of the cast will be from The Archway Theatre Youth Workshop.

We look forward to seeing you at the audition.
