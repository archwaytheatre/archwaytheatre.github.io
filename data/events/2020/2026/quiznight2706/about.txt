Join us for a fun-filled pub quiz at the Archway Function Room, hosted by veteran quizmaster George Butcher! We’ve got a great mix of lighthearted brain teasers, fun trivia, music rounds and general knowledge questions. (And also one absolutely ridiculous question that no-one will get right but George finds funny.) 

Grab your friends, enjoy a great evening, and see if your team can top the leaderboard on 27th June!

Tickets £5. 7:00pm - 9:30pm. All proceeds to charity.
Tickets need to be purchased in advance. Ticket sales close 1 hour before the start.
