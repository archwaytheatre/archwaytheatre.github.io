It is probably fair to say that almost everyone knows the story of ‘A Christmas Carol’. Maybe you are a fan of the book, or have seen one of the numerous adaptations: Alistair Sim in ‘Scrooge’, Albert Finney in the musical, the cartoon with Mickey Mouse or Michael Caine with the Muppets - to name a few.

Well, this year is your chance to see our very own take on this timeless classic, live on our lovely little stage. There’s nothing quite like the magic of theatre to bring this story alive. With a spectacular set, colourful costumes and a host of memorable characters, this is guaranteed to send you home with a smile on your face and warmth in your heart.
