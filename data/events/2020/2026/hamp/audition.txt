
Hamp is a drama set during the battle of Passchendaele during World War I.

When Private Hamp, who is a simplistic and guileless youth and one of the smallest cogs in the war machine, crawls from a shell hole and walks away from battle, the army finds itself compelled to notice him. Cast will consist of 8+ All male with playing ages from 20 up to 60s

This play is a brutal and honest look at the extremity of what war demands of people not just at the front lines but those tasked with enforcing the laws and regulations that are part of war.
