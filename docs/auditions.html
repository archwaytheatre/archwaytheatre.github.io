Audition Notices

Clue

by Sandy Rustin adapted from the screenplay by Jonathan Lynn, additional material by Hunter Foster and Eric Price
directed by James Murtagh

Auditions:

7:45PM Tuesday, 28 January 2025

Playing dates: Wednesday, 30 April 2025 until Saturday, 10 May 2025

About the Production: 

Who? What? When? Where? How?!

Just some of the questions that arise when 6 seemingly random guests appear at a stately home for an evening of awkward pleasantries, light entertainment and oh yes, a murder!

If you have any questions about the audition or would like to borrow a script then please get in touch.

Roles:

  • Wadsworth - any age, male, a traditional English butler; the driving force of the play. Formal, quick-witted and very much trying to keep control of the unravelling proceedings.
  • Yvette - 20s-30s, female, the maid; loyal to Wadsworth and possible in over her head with this party.
  • Miss Scarlet - any age, female, a madam; a dry, sardonic businesswoman whose business is about finding out about yours.
  • Mrs Peacock - 30s+, female, the politician's wife; batty, neurotic and quick to hysteria.
  • Mrs White - 30s+, female, the frequent widow; surrounded by tragedy but how much of it did she cause?
  • Colonel Mustard - 30s+, male, the military man; a puffy, pompous, dense blowhard who - if bullets were knowledge - would be down to his last shot.
  • Professor Plum - 30s+, male, the academic; somewhat arrogant and easily impressed with himself but why is he no longer working in medicine?
  • Mr Green - any age, male, the bureaucrat; timid, accident-prone and very anxious - what is his secret?
  • - Ensemble (most only in one scene so doubling up most likely and being able to play dead is a bonus!)
    The Cook - 30s+, female, gruff with a threatening presence
    Mr Boddy - 30s+, male, the mysterious host
    The Motorist - 30s+, male, what a time to break down!
    Singing Telegram Girl - 20s-30s, female, here to lighten the mood
    An Unexpected Cop - 30s+, male, just checking in
    Chief of Police - 30s+, male, ready to save the day
    Backup Police officers - any age, any gender
N.B.
  • Ages are a rough guide.
