Audition Notices

Vocation

by Lucy Miles
directed by Robert Gregory and Lucy Miles

Auditions:

2:00PM Sunday, 28 June 2026, in The Bar, Main Audition
7:30PM Monday, 29 June 2026, in The Bar, Callbacks

Playing dates: Thursday, 10 September 2026 until Saturday, 12 September 2026
Performing in the Studio Theatre

About the Production:

Written by Lucy Miles and directed by Robert Gregory and Lucy Miles, this play is to be staged over five performances in the Archway Studio.

What does vocation mean in a changing NHS? Find out in this bittersweet and relevant comedy. Old School Surgery is a chaotic GP practice, struggling to meet the needs (and demands!) of its patients, and run by an overstretched but committed workforce who sometimes adopt unorthodox strategies to cope.

Enter Kiera – an idealistic trainee doctor who is excitedly embarking on a career in general practice...

We are really excited to be staging the latest play written by Lucy, a practising doctor, which has been workshopped and further developed over recent months with the support of various members of the Archway family. We expect to commence rehearsals on 14th July, which are likely to be on Tuesdays, Thursdays and some Sundays, subject to agreement with the cast.

The play takes place in a GP surgery set in the current day.

If you are interested and would like to see a copy of the script, learn more about the individual characters, and hopefully take part in the audition, please contact Robert Gregory on robert.gregory@archwaytheatre.co.uk or Lucy Miles on lucyjmiles09@gmail.com.

Please note that Robert and Lucy intend to independently stage an additional single performance of the play the week following the Archway Studio run, on the evening of Tuesday 15th September at Guildford Fringe Festival. They are therefore looking for actors who are prepared to cover the five Archway performances and the additional one at Guildford Fringe Festival.

Audition pieces can be viewed at and downloaded from: Google Drive
