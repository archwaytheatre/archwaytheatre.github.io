Audition Notices

Hamp

by John Wilson
directed by James Abbott

Auditions:

7:30PM Monday, 03 August 2026, in The Function Room

Playing dates: Thursday, 26 November 2026 until Saturday, 28 November 2026
Performing in the The Studio

About the Production:

Hamp is a drama set during the battle of Passchendaele during World War I.

When Private Hamp, who is a simplistic and guileless youth and one of the smallest cogs in the war machine, crawls from a shell hole and walks away from battle, the army finds itself compelled to notice him. He is court-martialled for Desertion. While his defence, Lieutenant Hargreaves, and the Padre and others

Cast will consist of 8+ All male with playing ages from 20 up to 60s

This play is a brutal and honest look at the extremity of what war demands of people not just at the front lines but those tasked with enforcing the laws and regulations that are part of war. Please email me if you would like to find out more about the play, or have a look at the script. At the audition we will be doing sections of the script that allow characters to interact and show their depth.

Jdabbott177@gmail.com


Roles:

  • Private Arthur Hamp - Early 20s, male, A simplistic young soldier from Lancashire, who, under extreme stress, deserts.
  • Lieutenant William Hargreaves - 20s-40s, male, A well-educated officer from Lancashire tasked with the defence of Hamp.
  • Lieutenant Tom Webb - 20s-40s, male, Another Lancashire officer with a much more black-and-white view of the affair. Close friend of Hargreaves.
  • Prosecuting Officer Midgley - no specific age, male, Appointed to the prosecution of Hamp, sees Hargreaves’ efforts as a waste of time and resources.

May be doubled

  • Padre - 40+, male, The Chaplain of the regiment who gives spiritual advice to both Hamp and Hargreaves and testimony for defence.
  • Court President - 40+, male, Oversees the court-martial of Hamp.
  • Guard Corporal - 30+, male, A sympathetic soldier tasked with Hamp’s care.
  • Guard Private - 20+, male, direct subordinate to Corporal tasked with the same.
  • Medical Officer - 40+, male, An overstressed doctor who previously treated Hamp for ‘Nerves’.
N.B.
  • Ages are a rough guide.
