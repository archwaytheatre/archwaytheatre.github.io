3 Dec - 13 Dec │ Main House

A Vicar of Dibley Christmas The Second Coming 

The Christmas season is one to be enjoyed with family and friends and what better way to enjoy this magical season than in the company of our very own Vicar of Dibley and her fellow villagers.

Enjoy as the village broadcast for the very first time their own radio station “Radio Dibley” and prepare for the penultimate Christmas Nativity play.

The Archway Theatre brings back all the loveable characters of Dibley and can’t wait to kick start your Christmas festivities. Merry Christmas everyone.

20 Dec │ The Studio

The Dingies 2024 

Join us at 'The Dingies 2024' for an improv soirée like no other proudly presented by Dingbats Improv and The Archway Theatre!

Prepare for an evening of entertaining improv comedy scenes, stories and songs from the participants of the Dingbats Improv workshops in a fun and friendly improv jam!

Beyond the jam, we will bestow the prestigious Dingie awards upon those who have demonstrated unparalleled commitment to the craft in 2024.

There will be plenty of Academy Award winning scenes, Booker Prize winning stories, and Fashion Award winning gladrags... clad yourself in your most glamorous garments and we'll see you on the red carpet!

10 Jan - 25 Jan │ Main House

Snow White 

Who is the fairest of them all?

The Archway Theatre presents the magical tale of Snow White!

Queen Asphyxia rules her kingdom, believing she is the fairest of them all—until her enchanted mirror declares Snow White the new fairest.

Join Snow White, Prince Sebastian, Gertie the Greengrocer, Marsha Mellow, and the mischievous Jimmy Jester as they stand against the wicked plans of Slick Sly, Shifty, and the jealous Queen.

Meet the lovable Super Seven, who befriend Snow White on her journey in this laughter-filled, action-packed pantomime, guaranteed to delight audiences of all ages.
